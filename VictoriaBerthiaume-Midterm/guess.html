Guess I'll Die

Guess I’ll Die refers to a stock photo of a man in a red sweatshirt shrugging his shoulders. The image grew into a reaction image used in situations where the poster jokingly resigns themselves to death due to a minor inconvenience.

Origin

On the 6th of October, 2009, the photo was uploaded to iStockphoto by Ann Baldwin. It would later be revealed that the photo is of her husband, Mike Baldwin

Spread

The photo was used as a reaction image as early as April 2011 in a FARK.com forum thread on the film Atlas Shrugged. An edit of the image in which the man’s face is replaced with a sloth appeared online on the site herbatka.soup.io on the 18th of January, 2013.In 2013, the image began being used as a reaction image more frequently. It appeared in a Funnyjunk thread on April 20th, 2013, and again in a blog post on markb4.wordpress[6] on the 25th. It found continued use as a reaction image expressing confusion in the following years. A template for “shrugging old man” was added to Meme Generator on May 22nd, 2015. In September of 2015, it began going viral on Tumblr. A post by user noxyouraveragefangirl, now cinnamon-swan, paired it with a text post that gained over 207,000 notes
